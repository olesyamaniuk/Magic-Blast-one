Game Description

  • Enjoy a variety of enchanting themes, from mystical forests to magical castles, while unraveling secrets hidden in the puzzles.

  • Magic Blast: Mystery Puzzle is the ultimate combination of fun, strategy, and mystery, designed to captivate players of all ages.

Unique Features

  • Engaging Gameplay

    Magic Blast offers an innovative blend of match-and-blast mechanics with puzzle-solving elements. Players must clear cubes strategically, using power-ups and combos to tackle challenging levels while unraveling hidden mysteries

  • Variety of Levels and Themes

    From mystical forests to enchanting castles, each level features a unique design and objective. The ever-changing themes and puzzles ensure that no two levels feel the same, keeping players intrigued and entertained

  • Power-Ups and Boosters

    Utilize magical power-ups and boosters to clear difficult levels, blast through obstacles, and achieve high scores. Unlock special tools as you progress to master advanced strategies and outsmart challenges

  • Regular Updates and Events

    Participate in seasonal events, earn exclusive rewards, and discover new content regularly added to the game. Magic Blast ensures a fresh and dynamic gaming experience for its players

How to Play

  • Tap two or more adjacent cubes of the same color to blast them and clear the board

  • Strategically use power-ups and boosters to overcome challenging puzzles or reach level-specific goals

  • Complete objectives like collecting items, achieving target scores, or clearing specific blocks

  • Advance through levels with increasing difficulty, unlocking new themes and challenges

  • Participate in special events for extra rewards and unique puzzles

  • Manage your moves and think ahead to maximize your success in each level
